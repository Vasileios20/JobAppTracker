Where Your Career Commits Meet Opportunity Pushes

Welcome to GitHired, we help you fork your job search and merge it with success!

Whether you're actively pushing out applications or just committing time to refine your coding skills, GitHired is your all-in-one solution to stay organized, sharpen your technical chops, and prepare for interviews. Think of us as the GitHub of your career—except instead of repositories, you’re tracking job applications (though we fully support you branching out!).
