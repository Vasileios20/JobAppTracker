About GitHired, the Job Tracker and Code Challenge Platform

Welcome to GitHired, where we help you fork your job search and merge it with success! Whether you're actively pushing out applications or just committing time to refine your coding skills, GitHired is your all-in-one solution to stay organized, sharpen your technical chops, and prepare for interviews. Think of us as the GitHub of your career—except instead of repositories, you’re tracking job applications (though we fully support you branching out!).

Features

Job Application Tracker

No more job search spaghetti code! Our platform includes a comprehensive tracking tool to keep your job hunt organized and bug-free:

  • Track job applications: Keep a clean commit history of all your applications—job title, company, date applied, and status (because job hunting shouldn't be a mystery branch!).
  • Organize your search: Categorize by job type, industry, or even what stage you’re in (pending pull request a.k.a. interview?).
  • Monitor performance: Visualize success and response rates with dashboards, because who doesn't love a little data visualization? Bonus: unlock achievements along the way.
  • Set goals and streaks: Keep your job search productive with streaks and built-in milestones. It’s like version control for your motivation!

Code Challenges

Polish your coding skills before your next pull request (a.k.a. interview). Our platform offers a collection of LeetCode-style challenges to help you level up:

  • Real-world problem-solving: Face coding challenges based on real scenarios, not just another “FizzBuzz.”
  • Language support: Choose from a range of languages—Python, JavaScript, C++, and more. Pick your weapon of choice.
  • Gemini AI feedback: Submit your code and let our AI give you a review (like getting a pull request review, but with less snark). Find out what’s working and what needs refactoring.
  • Hybrid evaluation system (coming soon): Soon, you’ll get both AI and human code reviews, because sometimes even your AI could use a code buddy!

Interview Preparation

Get ready to ace that technical interview like a well-structured algorithm:

  • AI-powered interview practice: Simulate realistic interviews with AI-driven questions. No interviewer judgment if you need a second to Google (just kidding, no Googling allowed!).
  • Code challenges: Practice questions similar to those found in actual technical interviews, so you’re always prepared when the whiteboard comes calling.
  • Track progress: Keep tabs on how you’re improving—because success isn’t just about getting the job; it’s about growth. Version 2.0 of you is looking pretty good!

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help you pull success into your main branch. By combining job tracking, coding practice, and interview preparation, we provide a platform that makes the job search process less daunting and a little more fun—because we know job hunting can sometimes feel like an infinite loop.

Future Plans

We’re always iterating and building new features. Here’s what we’ve got coming up in our next release:

  • Enhanced coding challenge evaluation: AI and human-reviewed feedback to ensure your code gets the attention it deserves (no merge conflicts here).
  • Job suggestions: Personalized job recommendations based on your skills and history. It’s like auto-complete, but for your career!
  • Gamification: Earn points, badges, and other fun rewards for tracking applications and completing coding challenges. Because why shouldn’t job hunting be as rewarding as leveling up in your favorite game?

Thank you for choosing GitHired! We’re excited to help you rebase your career, one successful application at a time.
