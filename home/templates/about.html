About GitHired

Welcome to GitHired, an all-in-one tool designed to help you stay organized in your job search, improve your coding skills, and prepare for interviews. Whether you are actively applying for jobs or just practicing your technical skills, our platform offers a streamlined experience tailored to job seekers and developers alike.

Features

Job Application Tracker

Our platform includes a comprehensive job tracking tool, enabling users to:

  • Track job applications: Keep a record of all job applications, with details like job title, company, application date, and status.
  • Organize your search: Categorize applications based on job types, industries, and application stages.
  • Monitor performance: Visualize success rates, response rates, and track your progress through experience points and achievements.
  • Set goals and streaks: Stay motivated with built-in achievements and streaks to keep you on track.

Code Challenges

To help you sharpen your coding skills, we offer a vast collection of LeetCode-style coding challenges:

  • Real-world problem-solving: Practice solving coding problems based on real-world scenarios.
  • Language support: Choose from popular programming languages like Python, JavaScript, C++, and more.
  • Gemini AI feedback: After submitting your solution, receive AI-generated feedback to help you understand your strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Hybrid evaluation system (coming soon): We are developing a hybrid evaluation system that combines both AI feedback and traditional code evaluation, providing a balanced analysis of your code.

Interview Preparation

Prepare for technical interviews with:

  • AI-powered interview practice: Use the built-in AI to generate realistic interview questions and simulate interview scenarios.
  • Code challenges: Practice coding questions that are similar to those asked in technical interviews.
  • Track progress: Monitor your interview preparation journey with our performance tracking tools.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify and enhance the job application process while empowering you to improve your technical skills. By combining job tracking, coding practice, and interview preparation, we aim to provide a platform that prepares you for success in every step of your career journey.

Future Plans

We’re continuously working on new features to make the platform more robust and user-friendly. Upcoming features include:

  • Enhanced coding challenge evaluation with both AI and human-reviewed feedback.
  • Job suggestions based on your skills and job application history, powered by the Adzuna API.
  • Gamification of the job application and coding process, making it more interactive and rewarding.

Thank you for choosing GitHired. We’re excited to be part of your journey!
